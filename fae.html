Cursed Fae

A Dark Fantasy Romance

When Meg falls into a vortex in a tree, she is transported to a world she thought was in her dreams. As she travels through this new world, Meg finds others like her. People from her world who fell into a vortex. Only they learn they were called into Flouria for a reason. They are children of Seelie Fae and are the balance between good and evil. The six of them must find The Lost Land, harness their power, connect to their familiar all before the Unseelie drain Flouria of the magic and turn everything into blood and ash.

Tropes: Fae Romance, Fated Mates, Powerful Magic, Powerful Familiars, Children of Prophecy, Good vs Evil, Found Family

The Lost Land

Buy Now

After falling into a new world, Meg learns she is a key to saving it.
Flouria was a fairytale world that Meg thought she imagined to cope with the realities of her life. She was transported to the world she created after discovering a mysterious tree. Teleporting to another realm is one thing, but coming face to face with a creepy mist and its deadly creatures is another. While fighting for her life, Meg meets others like her—changelings who were brought back to the realm, and they all feel she is the missing Princess who could save the realm. The clues all point to them finding The Lost Land to find the answers they seek on how to fight against the Unseelie's nightmarish horde and save the realm. While the group travels through the realm, the Unseelie King and Queen have learned of her return and are planning her demise. Will they make it in time? Or will the evil mist get a hold of them first?

The Long Road

Six Seelie Guardians. The Unseelie Mist destroys the realm. A race against time.
Meg and her fellow Guardians now have the ability to restore Flouria to its former glory, or so they hope. With their magic unlocked, they hope to fix what the Unseelie damaged with their destructive Mist. They have a taxing journey ahead of them to the castle, but that only gives them time to master their skills. With true Seelie magic being unleashed back into the world, King Dragus and Queen Maurelle are fighting to maintain their rule. Seelie magic is dying, with the Guardians fighting off the Mist and Unseelie Knights at every turn. Will they be able to get to the Unseelie King and Queen in time before the Seelie magic dies out entirely? They have a long road ahead of them…
